What is it about

POLS was my first real web application and I developed it for readers who would like to get their own libararies to store their books online,where they can upload their books for free and download them again whenever they want.

What If I do not have the book in my PC or mobile but I want to add it in my libarary

POLS allows you to download any book from any website and then easily add it to your library by uploading it.
